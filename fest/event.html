  1. QUIZ

    2. To all the aspirants in the arena of our technical symposium, Welcome all!!!!! We give you a platform to exhibit your talent in the core of department. People with quick impulse step in and walk away with exciting prizes in the Quiz event of C-Elit 2K19.

    Both technical and non-technical quiz will be conducted separately.
    Technical quiz will be conducted from the core topics of CSE.
    Non-Technical quiz will be conducted on general topics.
      Rules:
    • Two rounds will be conducted.
    • In the preliminary round, individual candidates will be participated and the candidates are shortlisted.
    • In the final round , the shortlisted candidates are formed as a group of two.
    • Every participant should carry a mobile phone with good internet connection.

  2. BLIND CODING:

    3. “If you trust your fingers then there is an event waiting for you”.
    Event details:

    • It contains two rounds.
    • In the first round ,you will be given a piece of code with little errors.
    • In the second round ,a question is given to you to solve.
    • The screen will be turnedoff.
    • There is a strict time limit.
    • Programming languages allowed are “C” and “JAVA”.
    • Participants with best accuracy wins.

  3. CODING AND DEBUGGING:

    4. ROUND 1:Debugging(Elimination Round)

    • A set of 2 questions will be given with some logical and syntactical errors.
    • Total points will be evaluated upon the correctness of the submitted programs.
    • Selected candidates will be promoted to 2nd round
    ROUND 2:Coding(Final Round)
    • A set of 2 questions will be given to each candidate .
    • Questions must be solved in C language only.
    • Depending on the correctness of the code, the winners will be decided.
