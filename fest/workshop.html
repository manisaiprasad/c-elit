Workshop


The workshop aims to bring together students and researchers in an attempt to build a vibrant acadamic community. the workshop includes leactures, hands-on tutorial sessions by renowned experts on the field. C-Elit conducts 2-day workshop on the following areas of intrest. Intestred participants may register for one of the following workshops. The seats are limited and the selection is on first come first serve basis.

  1. AWS (Amazon Web Services )

    2. Amazon Web Services provides on-demand computing platform to individuals, Companies and organizations on subscricption basics. The Subscribers may utilize services like cluster of computers , software , storage and other resoures with high availability. the workshop covers leactures and hands-on tutorial sessions on following topics

    • Introducon and History of AWS
    • AWS Foundaonal Services: EC2, VPC, S3, EBS
